Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our therapy services

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our online calendar. Simply select your preferred date and time, fill in your details, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email shortly after booking.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment up to 24 hours before your scheduled time. Please call us at 0707828164 or email somoerajab90@gmail.com to make changes. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance to secure your preferred time slot. However, we do accommodate last-minute appointments when availability permits. Check our live calendar for current availability.

How long is each therapy session?

Individual therapy sessions are 1 hour. Couples therapy and group counseling sessions are 1.5 hours. Corporate and institutional sessions vary based on the package (3-5 hours).

What happens during the first session?

Your first session is an opportunity for us to get to know each other. We'll discuss what brings you to therapy, your goals, and any concerns you have. It's also a chance for you to ask questions and see if we're a good fit. There's no pressure - you decide if you want to continue.

Do you offer online/virtual sessions?

Yes! We offer secure, confidential video therapy sessions for clients who prefer online counseling or are unable to attend in-person sessions. Simply indicate your preference when booking.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies depending on your goals and needs. Some clients benefit from short-term therapy (6-12 sessions), while others prefer longer-term support. We'll discuss this together and create a plan that works for you.

What are your rates?

  • Individual Therapy: Ksh 1,800 (1 hour)
  • Group Counseling: Ksh 3,500 (1.5 hours)
  • Marriage/Couples Therapy: Ksh 2,800 (1 hour)
  • Children/Teenage Counselling: Ksh 1,500 (1 hour)
  • Career Counselling: Ksh 1,800 (1 hour)
  • Psychological Assessments: Ksh 2,800 (1 hour)
  • Corporate Wellness: Ksh 27,500 (full day)

Do you accept insurance?

Currently, we do not process insurance claims directly. However, we can provide you with a receipt that you can submit to your insurance provider for potential reimbursement. Please check with your insurance company about out-of-network mental health benefits.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and cash payments. Payment is due at the time of service. Receipts are provided for all transactions.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. Confidentiality is the foundation of effective therapy. All your personal information and session content are kept strictly confidential, with exceptions only in cases of imminent harm or as required by law. We follow all KCPA and Kenya Psychological Association ethical guidelines.

Why do you need my ID number?

Your ID number is used for our confidential client records and verification purposes. It is stored securely and never shared with third parties. This is standard practice for professional psychological services in Kenya.
